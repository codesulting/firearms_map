  • Help spread the word on the Internet.
  • Help change the rhetoric on the gun forums from passive whining to a bias towards action. Instead of whining, what are you going to do about it? Share with others. They just might be inspired to act themselves.

How was this map created?

We downloaded a spreadsheet of FFL data for the state from ATF's website. We then curated the data by going through every row and deleting those that:

  • Did not have a business name entry or whose license name was a person's name and not a business's.
  • Belong to a big box store such was Walmart or Dick's.
  • Have a business name that implied the business doesn't necessarily cater to a majority pro-gun audience.
Once the data was curated, we used the code on our GitHub repository to convert the data from a spreadsheet to JSON so that it can be displayed on the map above.

Why is a gun shop, FFL, or club I know about not on the map?

Please see the answer to the question about how the map was created for more details. In short, our process for adding points to the map was intended to weed out cases where an FFL does not have a store front or does not cater to a majority pro-gun audience. For many reasons, this might have caused us to delete your favorite entity.

For example, sometimes people get an FFL without a business name, then create a business later. In these cases the FFL is assigned to an individual at their private residence. We have no way of knowing if those licensees want random people showing up at their house.

Similarly, many people own unrelated businesses such as construction, landscaping, jewelry, etc. that they got an FFL for reasons we can only guess at. Either way, we have no way to know if they have a store front catering to a majority pro-gun audience and therefore removed it.

If we removed any entry in error, send their business name, address, and phone number to info@pagunvote.com and we'll gladly add it. Same goes for sportsman's clubs.
